Frequently Asked Questions

Kashmir Trip — Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your Kashmir holiday.

What is the best time to visit Kashmir?+

Kashmir is beautiful year-round. Spring (March-May) is best for tulips and flowers. Summer (June-August) brings pleasant weather. Autumn (September-November) offers golden chinar trees. Winter (December-February) is perfect for snow and skiing in Gulmarg.

How much does a Kashmir tour package cost?+

Kashmir tour packages start from ₹12,999 per person for 4 nights/5 days, including hotel, private transport, sightseeing, shikara ride, and Gulmarg gondola. Custom packages are available based on your dates, group size and preferences.

Which are the must-visit places in Kashmir?+

Top places to visit: Dal Lake, Gulmarg, Pahalgam, Sonmarg, Nishat Garden, Tulip Garden (spring only), Betab Valley, Doodhpathri, Kokarnag, Verinag, Aharbal Waterfall and Ladakh — Leh, Nubra Valley & Pangong Lake. We cover all 12 major destinations across Kashmir & Ladakh.

Is Kashmir safe to visit?+

Yes, Kashmir is safe for tourists. Tourism is at an all-time high with thousands of visitors every month. Our local team provides 24/7 support and we follow all safety protocols recommended by the J&K Tourism Department.

How many days are needed for a Kashmir trip?+

5 to 7 days is ideal. 4N/5D covers Srinagar, Gulmarg and Pahalgam. 5N/6D adds Sonmarg. 7N/8D allows offbeat destinations like Doodhpathri and Ladakh (Leh, Nubra Valley, Pangong Lake).

Do you provide airport pickup in Srinagar?+

Yes, all our Kashmir packages include airport pickup and drop in Srinagar with a private vehicle. Our local team meets you at arrival and assists throughout your trip.

What is included in your Kashmir tour packages?+

All packages include: Hotel accommodation (3-star or 4-star as chosen), private vehicle for all transfers, airport pickup/drop, sightseeing with local guide, shikara ride on Dal Lake, Gulmarg gondola tickets, and all applicable taxes.
